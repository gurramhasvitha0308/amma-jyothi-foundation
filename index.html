Who We Are

Our Core Origin & Purpose

Established in Hyderabad on July 8, 2022, Amma Jyothi Foundation was founded to bring dignity, nutrition, and comfort to seniors lacking family care. We strive to create supportive communities across Telangana through holistic care, education, and development.
