About Our Foundation

Driven by compassion, structured for accountability, and active in local development.

Our Founding Story

Amma Jyothi Foundation was established on July 8, 2022, as a registered society in Hyderabad, Telangana. The foundation arose from a compassionate urge to support senior citizens lacking family care, shelter, or financial security.

Our active outreach began in Saidabad and expanded as we recognized the interconnected needs of rural and urban blocks. Today, our operations serve families across Hyderabad, Rangareddy, and Medchal-Malkajgiri districts.

📅

July 2022

Registered as an official society (No. 222 of 2022) with the Registrar of Societies in Hyderabad.

👵

Late 2022

Launched local elder nutrition programs and companionship visits in Saidabad, Hyderabad.

🗺️

2023 - Present

Expanded operational coverage to include rural Rangareddy and Medchal-Malkajgiri districts.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To restore dignity, safety, and care for vulnerable elderly citizens while driving education, health, and skill initiatives that lift families out of structural poverty.

👁 Our Vision

A compassionate society where seniors live with dignity and respect, children have access to quality education, and marginalized communities are empowered to thrive.
